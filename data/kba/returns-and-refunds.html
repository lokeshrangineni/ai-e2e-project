Returns and Refunds Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you are not happy with your order, we make it easy to return items and receive a refund.

Return Window

You may return most items within 30 days of the delivery date for a full refund. Electronics must be returned within 15 days of delivery. Items marked as Final Sale are not eligible for return.

Important: Items must be in their original condition — unused, unwashed, and with all original tags and packaging intact.

How to Initiate a Return

  1. Log in to your ShopChat account and navigate to My Orders.
  2. Select the order containing the item you want to return.
  3. Click Request Return and select the reason for your return.
  4. Print the prepaid return shipping label that will be emailed to you.
  5. Pack the item securely and drop it off at any authorized carrier location.

You can also initiate a return by contacting our support team via chat and providing your order number.

Refund Timelines

Refund method Processing time
Original payment method (credit/debit card) 5–7 business days after we receive the item
ShopChat store credit 1–2 business days after we receive the item
PayPal 3–5 business days after we receive the item

You will receive an email confirmation once your return is processed and the refund has been issued.

Non-Returnable Items

  • Final Sale items (marked at time of purchase)
  • Digital downloads and software licenses
  • Personalized or custom-made products
  • Opened consumable items (e.g., batteries, ink cartridges)
  • Items showing signs of use, damage, or missing original packaging

Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery. We will arrange a free return and send a replacement at no extra cost, or issue a full refund — whichever you prefer. Photographs of the damage may be required to process your claim.

Exchanges

We do not offer direct exchanges at this time. To get a different size, color, or product, please return the original item and place a new order. If you use ShopChat store credit for the refund, the credit will be available within 1–2 business days and can be applied immediately to your new order.
