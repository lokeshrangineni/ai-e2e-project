Product Warranty and Guarantees

ShopChat stands behind the quality of everything we sell. Most products come with a manufacturer's warranty, and we offer our own ShopChat Guarantee on top of that.

ShopChat Quality Guarantee

If any product you purchase from ShopChat fails due to a manufacturing defect within the warranty period, we will repair it, replace it, or give you a full refund — your choice. This guarantee is in addition to any manufacturer warranty.

Warranty Periods by Category

Product category ShopChat Guarantee Typical manufacturer warranty
Electronics 1 year 1–2 years (varies by brand)
Footwear 6 months 90 days (varies by brand)
Clothing and Apparel 6 months Not typically offered
Accessories 6 months 90 days (varies by brand)
Home and Kitchen 1 year 1 year (varies by brand)

What is Covered

  • Manufacturing defects (e.g., a zipper that breaks after 2 months of normal use)
  • Materials failures (e.g., stitching coming apart under normal conditions)
  • Electronic malfunctions not caused by user damage (e.g., a device that randomly shuts off)
  • Structural defects discovered after delivery

What is Not Covered

  • Normal wear and tear (e.g., worn-down shoe soles after 12 months of daily use)
  • Accidental damage (e.g., a cracked phone screen)
  • Damage from misuse or unauthorized modifications
  • Cosmetic damage that does not affect functionality (e.g., minor scratches)
  • Items damaged by water unless they are rated as water-resistant or waterproof
  • Products purchased from third-party sellers (warranty is the seller's responsibility)
Note: The ShopChat Guarantee and manufacturer warranties do not cover damage caused by misuse, accidents, or unauthorized repairs.

How to File a Warranty Claim

  1. Log in to your account and go to My Orders.
  2. Select the order containing the defective item.
  3. Click Report a Problem and select Warranty Claim.
  4. Describe the defect and upload photos showing the issue.
  5. Our team will review your claim within 3 business days.
  6. If approved, we will contact you to arrange repair, replacement, or refund.

You can also start a warranty claim by chatting with the ShopChat assistant and mentioning your order number and the issue you're experiencing.

Extended Warranty

At checkout, you can purchase an extended warranty for eligible Electronics and Home products. Extended warranties add 1 or 2 additional years of coverage beyond the standard period and include accidental damage protection. Extended warranty plans are administered by our partner ProtectPlus and can be managed from Account Settings → My Warranties.
