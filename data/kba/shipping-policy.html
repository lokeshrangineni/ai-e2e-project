Shipping Policy and Delivery Times

We offer a range of shipping options to suit your needs. All orders are processed within 1–2 business days of being placed, excluding weekends and public holidays.

Free standard shipping on all orders over $50. No promo code needed — discount is applied automatically at checkout.

Shipping Options

Shipping method Estimated delivery Cost

Express Shipping 2–3 business days $12.99

International Standard 10–21 business days Calculated at checkout

Order Processing

Orders placed before 2:00 PM EST Monday through Friday are typically processed the same day. Orders placed after 2:00 PM EST or on weekends will be processed the next business day. During peak periods (e.g., holidays), processing times may extend by 1–2 business days.

Order Tracking

Once your order ships, you will receive a shipping confirmation email with a tracking number. You can track your order by:

  • Logging in to your account and visiting My Orders
  • Clicking the tracking link in your shipping confirmation email
  • Asking the ShopChat assistant: "Where is my order?" with your order number

Shipping Restrictions

We currently ship to all 50 US states, US territories (Puerto Rico, Guam, USVI), and over 40 international countries. The following items cannot be shipped internationally:

  • Lithium battery products (certain electronics)
  • Aerosol products
  • Items subject to country-specific import restrictions

Missing or Lost Packages

If your tracking shows "delivered" but you have not received your package, please:

  1. Check around your delivery location and with neighbors.
  2. Wait 24 hours — some carriers mark packages as delivered slightly before arrival.
  3. Contact our support team if the package is still missing after 24 hours.

We will open a trace with the carrier. If the package cannot be located, we will send a replacement or issue a full refund within 5 business days.

Address Changes

If you need to change a shipping address, contact us immediately. We can only update the address before the order is picked up by the carrier. Once the order is in transit, we are unable to redirect it.
