Account Management and Security

Your ShopChat account lets you track orders, manage payment methods, and update your personal information. Here's how to stay in control of your account.

Updating Your Profile

To update your name, email address, or phone number:

  1. Log in to your account.
  2. Click your name in the top-right corner and select Account Settings.
  3. Edit the relevant fields and click Save Changes.

Email address changes require verification. A confirmation link will be sent to your new email address, and your old address will receive a notification of the change.

Resetting Your Password

If you have forgotten your password or want to change it:

  1. Go to the login page and click Forgot Password.
  2. Enter your registered email address and click Send Reset Link.
  3. Check your inbox for a password reset email (check your spam folder if it doesn't arrive within 5 minutes).
  4. Click the reset link — it is valid for 2 hours.
  5. Enter and confirm your new password.

To change your password while logged in, go to Account Settings → Security → Change Password.

Password requirements: At least 8 characters, including one uppercase letter, one lowercase letter, one number, and one special character.

Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

We strongly recommend enabling 2FA to protect your account. ShopChat supports authenticator apps (Google Authenticator, Authy) and SMS-based 2FA.

Enabling 2FA

  1. Go to Account Settings → Security → Two-Factor Authentication.
  2. Choose your preferred method: Authenticator App or SMS.
  3. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete setup.
  4. Save your backup codes in a safe place — these can be used if you lose access to your 2FA device.

Resetting 2FA

If you have lost access to your authenticator app or phone number:

  1. On the login page, click Can't access your 2FA device?
  2. Enter one of your saved backup codes.
  3. Once logged in, go to Account Settings → Security to reconfigure 2FA.

If you no longer have backup codes, contact our support team. You will be asked to verify your identity before 2FA is reset.

Never share your 2FA codes or backup codes with anyone, including ShopChat support staff. We will never ask for them.

Managing Saved Addresses

You can save multiple shipping addresses to your account for faster checkout. Go to Account Settings → Addresses to add, edit, or delete saved addresses. Mark one address as your default to have it pre-selected at checkout.

Closing Your Account

To permanently close your account, go to Account Settings → Privacy → Close Account. You will be asked to confirm the action by entering your password. Note that:

  • All personal data will be deleted within 30 days per our Privacy Policy.
  • Order history will be retained for 7 years for tax and legal compliance, but will be anonymized.
  • Any unused store credit or active gift cards will be forfeited.

Account closure is permanent and cannot be undone. If you want to return to ShopChat in the future, you will need to create a new account.
