Payment Methods and Billing

ShopChat supports a variety of payment methods to make checkout as convenient as possible.

Accepted Payment Methods

Method Details
Credit cards Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover
Debit cards Visa and Mastercard debit cards with a billing address
PayPal Pay with your PayPal balance, linked bank account, or PayPal credit
ShopChat Store Credit Issued for returns; applied automatically at checkout
Gift Cards Enter your gift card code at checkout; balance applied before other payment
Buy Now Pay Later Klarna — split into 4 interest-free payments (subject to eligibility)

We do not currently accept cryptocurrency, wire transfers, checks, or cash on delivery.

Managing Saved Payment Methods

To add, edit, or remove a saved card:

  1. Log in and go to Account Settings → Payment Methods.
  2. Click Add Payment Method to add a new card, or click the three-dot menu next to an existing card to edit or delete it.
  3. Set a card as default to have it pre-selected at every checkout.
We use industry-standard encryption (PCI-DSS Level 1). We never store your full card number — only the last 4 digits and expiry date are visible in your account.

Understanding Your Invoice

After each order is placed, you will receive an email receipt. It includes:

  • Itemized list of products, quantities, and prices
  • Shipping cost and method
  • Any discount codes or store credit applied
  • Sales tax (calculated based on your shipping address)
  • Total charged and the last 4 digits of the card used

To download an invoice for a past order, go to My Orders → select the order → Download Invoice.

Billing Disputes and Unauthorized Charges

If you see a charge from ShopChat that you do not recognize:

  1. Check your order history — charges may appear a day or two after your order is placed.
  2. Ask the ShopChat assistant or contact support with the charge amount and date.
  3. If you believe the charge is unauthorized, contact your bank immediately and then contact us so we can investigate.

We respond to all billing disputes within 2 business days.

Sales Tax

Sales tax is calculated at checkout based on your shipping address and applicable state and local tax rates. Tax-exempt organizations can apply for tax exemption by contacting our support team with their exemption certificate.

Promotional Codes and Discounts

Enter promo codes in the Promo Code field at checkout before placing your order. Promo codes:

  • Cannot be applied after an order is placed
  • Cannot be combined with other promo codes (one code per order)
  • Can be combined with store credit and gift cards
  • Are case-insensitive
